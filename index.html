My name is Edder Martinez and I am a current senior at University of California Riverside. I am from East Los Angeles, California and proud to represent my community in LA. Some of the things I enjoy are playing video games, working out, and watching/playing football. I am an education major and I plan on continuing my education to get my masters in education.

I am currently a high school football coach at my alma mater because I am in love with the sport and I want to help my community out any way possible. One thing I noticed is that a lot of students, especially in my neighborhood, have big dreams of playing for a university and potentially making it to a professional league. I plan on getting students to their dream by helping more students of color go out to college to extend their education and to also make a positive impact on the world.

I have had my fair share of teachers who were not so good, in which resulted me getting bad grades and find learning not interesting. I plan ot break that cycle by being professional at my job while also being able to connect with my students and being able to teach with popular things happening in teenager's lives. This will help make learning much more interesting and fun for students. It will also help to get them to learn about new things they did not know that is happening around the world.
